## Our Vision

In today's society, Artificial Intelligence is fundamentally reshaping how we live and work. It is often viewed with apprehension, cited as a contributor to reduced attention spans in children who delegate their problem-solving to machines, and a source of potential misinformation. Yet, the harsh truth is undeniable: **AI is here to stay.**

> "In an AI-driven landscape, English is the ultimate tool for adaptation. To harness these platforms effectively, you must master the language that controls them."

To succeed, students must master three crucial skill sets that go beyond traditional ESL:

**Asking the Right Questions**
As the saying goes, "You don't know what you don't know." If a student lacks the English proficiency to prompt a Large Language Model (LLM) correctly, they cannot extract accurate results.

**Articulating with Precision**
Students must be able to express their thoughts with absolute clarity to direct AI tools efficiently and get satisfying, useful outputs.

**Critical Defense**
Deep language comprehension is the foundation of critical thinking, allowing students to identify AI "hallucinations" (fabricated responses) and counteract algorithmic bias.

**This is the core mission of the DK English School.** We utilize research-verified curricula combined with an instructional "scaffolding" method. By breaking down complex language acquisition into manageable steps, we reduce student stress, make learning deeply interactive, and maximize students' ability to thrive as critical thinkers.
